🔎 How the numbers are calculated ▶

Levers 1 / 4 / 6 (rate uplifts): loss reduction = earned premium × uplift

Levers 2 / 5 (loss-ratio improvement): loss reduction = earned premium × (current LR − target LR)

Lever 3 (expense-ratio reduction): saving = written premium × (current ER − target ER)

Total loss reduction = sum of individual levers × 0.85 (cross-lever overlap factor)

New loss ratio = (original claims − claim-side savings + new claims from scaling) / (new total earned premium + new scaling premium)

New expense ratio = (original expense − expense-side savings + new expense from scaling) / (new total written premium + new scaling premium)

New COR = new loss ratio + new expense ratio + 6% (management expense)

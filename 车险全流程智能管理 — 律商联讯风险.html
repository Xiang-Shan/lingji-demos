A+BI CHALLENGES

Four Blind Spots of Traditional BI

When the business moves fast, the limits of traditional BI become painfully obvious.

01
Rigid by design
Every KPI and workflow must be defined up-front. The reporting layer cannot keep pace with a business that changes week-on-week.
The business responds to change slower than the market
02
Locked to pre-defined views
Pre-built dashboards answer only the questions they were designed for. Exploratory analysis means a new report request and a long wait.
Cannot answer the questions you didn't know to ask
03
Passive, backward-looking
A dashboard shows yesterday's numbers. It does not raise a hand when a risk signal or a growth opportunity appears in the data.
Opportunities and risks stay buried
04
Unstructured data wasted
Call recordings, claims photos, policy documents — the unstructured 80% often carries the most valuable signal, but traditional BI cannot read it.
The richest data is the data you're ignoring

CHALLENGES

Three hard challenges of motor-insurance outbound sales

01
Huge skill gap between agents
Conversion rates between your best and weakest agents differ by a factor of 3–5×. The team's ceiling is set by the top quartile, but the floor drags the average down.
Average conversion is held back by the bottom performers
02
Heavy staff turnover
Outbound teams churn at industry-leading rates. Every leaver takes tacit skill and customer knowledge with them; the team never compounds.
Know-how walks out the door with every leaver
03
High cost of training & QA
New hires ramp slowly, compliance monitoring relies on spot-checks, and leadership has no real-time view of call quality across the floor.
Management time is eaten by low-value oversight
